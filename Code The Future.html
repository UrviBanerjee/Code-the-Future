How It Works

As we're just starting up, Code the Future is offering our first projects completely free to both students and business partners. This allows us to build our portfolio, refine our processes, and demonstrate the value we can provide. After this initial phase, we'll transition to our paid model where students can earn while they learn by working on real client projects.

Our unique approach combines technical education with business acumen, giving students not just coding skills but also the ability to communicate with clients, understand business needs, and deliver professional solutions.
